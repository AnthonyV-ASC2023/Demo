IT'S GETTING TOO DANG HOT

Now what are large scale actions that are possible to slow down global warming?

Solution

Transition to Renewable Energy: Shift from fossil fuels to renewable energy sources like solar, wind, hydro, and geothermal power. This reduces greenhouse gas emissions from energy production.

Energy Efficiency: Improve energy efficiency in buildings,transportation, and industries to reduce overall energy consumption and emissions.

Reforestation and Afforestation: Planting trees and restoring forests helps absorb CO2 from the atmosphere and enhances carbon sinks.

Sustainable Land Use: Adopt sustainable agricultural practices, protect natural habitats, and avoid deforestation to maintain carbon storage and biodiversity.

Reduce Emissions from Transportation: Promote electric vehicles, public transportation, biking, and walking to reduce emissions from the transportation sector.

Waste Reduction and Recycling: Minimize waste production, promote recycling, and implement efficient waste management practices to reduce methane emissions from landfills.
