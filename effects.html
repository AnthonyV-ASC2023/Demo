IT'S GETTING TOO DANG HOT

What exactly are the effects of all of these causes?

Effects

Temperature rise: Earth's average surface temperature increases due to greenhouse gas emissions.

Melting ice and rising sea levels: Polar ice melts, leading to higher sea levels and coastal risks.

Extreme weather events: More frequent and intense hurricanes, cyclones, droughts, floods, and wildfires.

Ocean acidification: CO2 absorption leads to acidification, harming marine life and ecosystems.

Biodiversity loss: Climate changes contribute t o species extinction and habitat shifts.

Agriculture and food security: Altered climate affects crop yields and food production.

Human health impacts: Heat-related illnesses and the spread of diseases may increase.

Economic and social consequences: Disruptions, displacement, and migration challenges arise.

Mitigation: Addressing global warming requires reducing emissions and adapting to changes.
